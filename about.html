ABOUT US

PinguStationery와 계속 연결하세요.

Pingu Stationery Shop began as a small dream on a quiet street corner, where soft pastel colors met the gentle rhythm of everyday life. The founder, inspired by childhood memories of doodling in the margins of notebooks and collecting tiny, meaningful objects, wanted to create a space where people could slow down and rediscover simple joys.

At the heart of the shop is Pingu, a tiny penguin mascot who loves to write letters, sketch little stories, and keep memories safe on paper. Every item in the store—from smooth gel pens to carefully designed notebooks—is chosen with that same spirit: to make ordinary moments feel a little more special.

The shop isn’t loud or overwhelming. Instead, it feels calm and comforting, like a place you can wander into after a long day. Sunlight filters through the windows, landing softly on shelves lined with neatly arranged stationery. There’s always a quiet sense that each item is waiting for someone to give it a purpose—whether it’s writing a heartfelt note, planning a dream, or simply decorating a page. Pingu Stationery Shop isn’t just about buying things. It’s about creating a small world where thoughts, ideas, and memories can take shape—one page at a time.
